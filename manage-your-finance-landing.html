Manage Your Finance is the money tracker built for student budgets — irregular income, recurring small spends, and zero patience for spreadsheets.

No sign-up servers. No ads. No selling your spending habits.
What it does

Everything a student's wallet actually needs.

No investment portfolios. No business invoicing. Just the four things that matter when your money comes in bursts and goes out in coffee-sized increments.

📊

One dashboard, no guessing

Total balance, this month's income, this month's spend — all at a glance, the second you open the app.

➕

Log it in seconds

Add an expense or income entry with an amount, a category, and a date. That's it — no friction between spending and tracking it.

🎯

Budgets that push back

Set a monthly cap per category. Go near it and the bar turns gold. Go over it and the app tells you straight.

🥧

See the shape of your spending

A pie chart by category and a 6-month trend bar chart turn months of small transactions into one honest picture.

🍜

Categories built for student life

Food, Study, Transport, Entertainment, Rent, and more — already set up, no configuring required.

🔒

Private by default

Everything lives on your device in a local database. No account, no cloud sync, no one else ever sees your numbers.

Getting started

Three steps. No bank linking, no waiting.

01

Set up your profile

Add your name and, if you want, a 4-digit PIN. Nothing's sent anywhere — it's saved locally on your phone.

02

Log income and spending

Every time money moves, add a quick entry. Pick income or expense, a category, an amount, done in under ten seconds.

03

Set budgets, watch the trend

Cap your categories, then check the dashboard and reports whenever you want a read on the month so far.
